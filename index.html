About

From the grid to the classroom.

I grew up in West Oakland and went straight into the trades — climbing poles for PG&E as an IBEW lineman, wiring solar arrays, and learning what infrastructure actually looks like from 60 feet up.

Now I'm at Berkeley City College with a 4.0 GPA, a Phi Theta Kappa member on track to transfer into UC Berkeley's EECS program, and heading into a research internship at Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory this summer.

I've sat where most PCCD students sit. I know what it's like to navigate financial aid with no safety net, to commute across the district, and to build a future without a blueprint. That's exactly why I'm running.

Platform

What I'll fight for.

⚡

Transfer equity

Streamline articulation with UC and CSU systems so PCCD students don't lose credits or time on a path that should be clear.

📚

AI & technology access

Advocate for institutional AI tool access as a student equity issue — not a luxury, but infrastructure for learning.

🏭

Workforce alignment

Bridge the gap between CTE programs and Bay Area industry. Trade students and tech students deserve the same institutional support.

👥

Disability services

Expand DSPS funding and reduce wait times. Accommodations should be a right that's enforced, not a process that exhausts.

💰

Financial transparency

Push for clear, accessible reporting on how the district spends student fees and bond revenue.

🌱

Student housing & food

Support emergency housing and food programs. You can't study on an empty stomach or from a car.
